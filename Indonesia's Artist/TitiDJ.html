Titi DJ

Bio

Titi Dwi Jayanti (born May 27, 1966), known as Titi DJ, is an Indonesian pop diva, songwriter, model, talent show judge, and television personality. She was a judge on Indonesian Idol from season 1 to 5 and season 8. She is known for the song, “Sang Dewi”. She was the first Indonesian female singer to perform a concert at Marina Bay Sands in Singapore and the first Indonesian singer to make perform seven concerts in a four-day period. she was the first female coach to win The Voice Indonesia in 2019. Titi DJ's most successful album was Bahasa Kalbu (1999), which made her the biggest winner at the 3rd Anugerah Musik Indonesia Awards, with five wins including Album of the Year, Artist of the Year, and Songwriter of the Year.
