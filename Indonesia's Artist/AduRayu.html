Bio

Mikael Adam Bin Mohd Rafee Michel Lozach or Mikael Adam Lozach, better known as SonaOne is a French-Malaysian singer-songwriter and rapper.[1] He gained recognition through the songs "I Don't Care" with Mal Karmal, "No More" and with rapper Joe Flizzow "Havoc" and "Hello". In 2014, he won an award in the 29th Anugerah Juara Lagu. His song "No More" was awarded the best song in the 21st Anugerah Industri Muzik. It made him a composer who has won two major awards in the same year, with the other being Adnan Abu Hassan.
