Bio

Sri Rossa Roslaina Handiyani,also known as Rossa, also known Teteh Ocha by fans in Indonesia or Dato' Sri Rossa Roslaina in Malaysia (born 9 October 1978, in Sumedang, West Java, Indonesia), is an Indonesian singer and business woman. She remains one of the most popular artists in Indonesian and Malay-speaking countries, and one of the best selling Indonesian artist all of time based on album sales, selling over 10 million copies in the region.Her second compilation album, The Best of Rossa, ranked 10th on the List of Indonesian all time best selling albums and her solo album Love, Life & Music successfully broke the Indonesian Record World Museum (MURI) & & Indonesia Recording Industry Associationas (ASIRI) as the highest selling Indonesian album of all time with over 100.000 copies sold in a day.
