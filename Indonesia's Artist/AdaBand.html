Ada Band

Bio

ADA Band is an Indonesian pop rock band formed in Jakarta in 1996. The group currently consists of drummer Adhy, bassist Dika and guitarist Marshal. ADA Band are known for their hit singles, such as "Manusia Bodoh", "Yang Terbaik Bagimu", "Masih", "Seharusnya", "Ough" and "Bilakah". The first album titled Seharusnya (Should), released in 1997. Their 2004 work, Heaven of Love, was the band's key seller, going quadruple platinum in their home nation.
