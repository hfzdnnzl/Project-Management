Bio

Andmesh Kamaleng (born in Alor, 15 April 1997; age 22 years) is an Indonesian singer. He is the champion for the second season of searching for the Rising Star Indonesia singing talent that aired on RCTI television station in 2016-2017, with the final voting result of 80% in the Grand Final on March 27, 2017.
