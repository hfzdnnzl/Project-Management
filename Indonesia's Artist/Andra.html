Andra & The Backbone

Bio

Andra and the BackBone is a music group from Indonesia. There are three members, namely Dedy, Stevie, and Andra Junaidi. This music group was formed in 2007. The main song is Musnah, which was re-popularized by Mulan Jameela. His first album was Andra and the BackBone, released in 2007. Another hit from this album is Perfect which was re-popularized by Gita Gutawa, and other hits were Again And Again, And Sleep, Deepest, Sick, and Listen to Me. One of its members, Andra Junaidi, is one of the founders and a former member of the band Dewa 19.
