Bio

AMoira Rachelle Bustamante Cruzado Dela Torre (born November 4, 1993)[2][3] is a Filipina singer-songwriter. She rose to fame for her covers of Imago's "Sundo", Moonstar88's "Torete", and the Himig Handog-winning single "Titibo-tibo". Malaya is the debut studio album by Filipina singer Moira Dela Torre. It was released in 2018 by Star Music.The album consists of thirteen tracks, ten original songs and three covers. The second single of the album of the same name, Malaya, was released on 18 September 2017.
