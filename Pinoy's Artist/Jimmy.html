Jimmy Bondoc

Bio

James Patrick Romero Bondoc (born May 31, 1975) is a Filipino pop singer. He studied from preparatory to college at the Ateneo de Manila University. Bondoc got his degree at the ADMU as an AB Communications graduate.[2] Bondoc is also now a freshman law student in San Beda College. Bondoc is an acoustic musician and has also composed and produced songs. Bondoc is known for his original hits "The Man I Was With You", "Akin Na Lang Sana Siya" and "Let Me Be The One."
